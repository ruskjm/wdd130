Nutrition

“Let thy food be thy medicine, which means our food should be our medicine and vice versa” - Hippocrates

Nutrition is a method in which the food is consumed by the organisms and utilizing the nutrients from the food. Nutrition is the process of taking in food and converting it into energy and other vital nutrients required for life. In the process of nutrition, organisms utilize nutrients.

It's not always easy to consume to correct foods for our body.

So what is best type of food to consume to give our bodies the proper nutrition?

We'll review the recommended foods to best meet our nutritional needs.

Healthy Eating Pyramid

Grains

It is recommended eating 5 to 8 ounces of grains per day, 3 to 6 ounces of which should be whole grains. For reference, one ounce of grains is equivalent to one slice of bread or half a cup of cooked pasta or rice.

Fruits and Vegetables

It is recommended eating 1 to 2 cups of fruits and 2 to 3 cups of vegetables daily.

Proteins

Anywhere from 10% to 35% of your calories should come from protein. So if your needs are 2,000 calories, that's 200-700 calories from protein, or 50-175 grams. The recommended dietary allowance to prevent deficiency for an average sedentary adult is 0.8 grams per kilogram of body weight.

Choose fish, poultry, beans, and nuts; limit red meat and cheese; avoid bacon, cold cuts, and other processed meats.

Fats, oils and sweets

The small tip of the Pyramid shows fats, oils, and sweets. These are foods such as salad dressings, cream, butter, margarine, sugars, soft drinks, candies, and sweet desserts. Alcoholic beverages are also part of this group. These foods provide calories but few vitamins and minerals.

Eat these food sparingly.
