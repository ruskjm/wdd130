Your Fitness Journey

Sometimes the journey to better fitness can be a little scary with many unknowns and challenges. This website will introduce you to the basics to get started. Those basics include body composition, nutrition and exercise.

Body Composition

When you start a journey you need a starting point. That starting point with fitness is to know your current body composition. Body composition refers to the percentage of fat, bone, and muscle in your body.
